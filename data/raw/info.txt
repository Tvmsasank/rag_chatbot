### IT Support Policy

Q: How do I raise an IT support ticket?
A: Employees can raise IT support tickets through the internal helpdesk portal or by emailing support@company.com.

Q: What should I do if my system is not working?
A: Restart the system and if the issue persists, raise a support ticket immediately.

Q: Is remote IT support available?
A: Yes, remote support is available during working hours.


### Work From Home Policy

Q: Is work from home allowed?
A: Work from home is allowed with prior approval from the reporting manager.

Q: How many days can I work from home?
A: Employees can work from home up to 2 days per week based on approval.

Q: Can I work remotely from another city?
A: Yes, but it requires special approval from HR and your manager.


### Security Policy

Q: What is the password policy?
A: Passwords must be at least 8 characters long and include uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters.

Q: How often should I change my password?
A: Passwords must be changed every 90 days.

Q: Can I share my login credentials?
A: No, sharing login credentials is strictly prohibited.


### Asset Management Policy

Q: What happens if I lose company laptop?
A: You must immediately report to IT and HR. Investigation will be conducted.

Q: Who is responsible for company assets?
A: Employees are fully responsible for the assets assigned to them.

Q: Can I use company laptop for personal use?
A: Limited personal use is allowed but should not violate company policies.


### Performance Evaluation Policy

Q: How is employee performance evaluated?
A: Performance is evaluated quarterly based on goals, teamwork, and productivity.

Q: What is the appraisal cycle?
A: Appraisals are conducted once a year.

Q: Can performance ratings be challenged?
A: Yes, employees can discuss ratings with their manager or HR.


### Leave Extension Policy

Q: Can I extend my leave?
A: Yes, leave can be extended with prior manager approval.

Q: What happens if I don’t return after leave?
A: It may be treated as unauthorized absence and can lead to disciplinary action.


### Exit Policy

Q: What is the process to resign?
A: Employees must submit resignation through the HR portal and inform their manager.

Q: What is full and final settlement?
A: It includes salary, leave balance, and other dues settled after exit.

Q: When will I receive my relieving letter?
A: Relieving letter is issued after completion of notice period and clearance.


### Code of Conduct

Q: What is expected employee behavior?
A: Employees must maintain professionalism, respect colleagues, and follow company rules.

Q: Is harassment allowed?
A: No, any form of harassment is strictly prohibited.

Q: What happens if policies are violated?
A: Disciplinary actions will be taken depending on severity.


### Meeting Policy

Q: Are meetings mandatory?
A: Yes, scheduled meetings must be attended unless approved otherwise.

Q: How should meetings be conducted?
A: Meetings should be time-bound, agenda-driven, and productive.

Q: Can meetings be recorded?
A: Yes, with consent of participants.


### Training Policy

Q: Are employees required to attend training?
A: Yes, mandatory training sessions must be completed.

Q: Are certifications sponsored?
A: Yes, company may sponsor certifications based on role relevance.

Q: What if I fail a training?
A: You may be required to retake the training.


Q: what is Company Leave Policy?
A: Employees get 24 days leave per year. Leave must be approved by manager.

Q: what is Travel Policy?
A: Only economy class flights allowed.

Q: what is HR Policy?
A: Every Employee must serve minimum of 30 days of notice period.

Q: Does the company provide equal opportunities?  
A: Yes, all employees and applicants are treated fairly regardless of race, gender, religion, age, or disability.

Q: What should I do in case of a workplace accident?  
A: Report immediately to your manager and HR. An incident response plan will be followed to ensure safety and compliance. 

Q: How is employee data protected?  
A: Employee data is stored securely and used only for legitimate business purposes. Sharing outside the company is prohibited without consent. 

Q: Can I post about work on social media?  
A: Employees may post responsibly but must not disclose confidential company information or misrepresent the organization. 

Q: How do I claim business expenses?  
A: Submit receipts through the expense portal within 30 days. Only pre-approved expenses will be reimbursed. 

Q: Do I need to disclose external business interests?  
A: Yes, employees must declare any external work or financial interests that may conflict with company responsibilities.

Q: How can I report unethical behavior?  
A: Employees can confidentially report misconduct through the whistleblower hotline or HR. Retaliation against whistleblowers is prohibited. 

Q: Is alcohol allowed during work hours?  
A: No, consumption of alcohol or drugs during work hours or on company premises is strictly prohibited.

Q: Who owns the work I create at the company?  
A: All work created during employment belongs to the company unless otherwise agreed in writing.

Q: How can I raise a workplace grievance?  
A: Employees can submit grievances to HR. The company ensures fair investigation and resolution within a set timeline.
